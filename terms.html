Terms of Service

Last updated: July 2026

1. Services

Blendz By Mora provides professional makeup artistry services including bridal, soft glam, and full glam makeup. Services are offered in the Las Vegas, Nevada area with optional travel arrangements available upon request.

2. Booking and Appointments

Appointments are requested through our website. When you submit a booking request, you must agree to our Service Agreement, which covers liability, allergies, travel, and payment terms. After you submit, you may pay your 50% deposit by card through Square’s secure payment form on our booking page. Your information is stored in our database (Supabase) and sent to us via email (Formspree). We may log the network address (IP) used for that submission as described in our Privacy Policy. Text messages (SMS) are only sent if you optionally opt in on the booking form (the checkbox is not required to submit a request). If you opt in, we may send SMS to the phone number you provide—such as a confirmation after your deposit is paid, a reminder about one day before your appointment, and prompts to confirm attendance (e.g. by replying YES). SMS is delivered by Twilio. See our Privacy Policy for details. We will contact you to confirm your appointment. Availability is subject to change.

3. Cancellation and Rescheduling

To cancel or reschedule, contact us at BlendzByMora@gmail.com at least 72 hours before your scheduled appointment.

If you cancel or request to reschedule with at least 72 hours’ notice, you may receive a full refund of your deposit or move your appointment to another mutually agreed date, subject to availability.

If you fail to attend your appointment (no-show), or if you cancel or request to reschedule with less than 72 hours’ notice, your 50% deposit will be forfeited. A forfeited deposit is non-refundable and may not be applied toward a future appointment or rescheduled date. See our Service Contract Template for full cancellation terms.

4. Payment

Blendz By Mora requires payment in two installments: a 50% deposit due at the time of booking to secure your appointment, and the remaining 50% balance due on the day of service, prior to the completion of your makeup services. For in-studio appointments, the balance is due when you arrive; for travel appointments, the balance is due on the day the Artist arrives at the agreed location. Services will not begin until the balance has been paid in full.

Deposits and card payments are processed securely through Square (including Square’s payment form on our booking page and Square invoices for the remaining balance). We also accept cash and Zelle for payment where agreed. Your total service fee is confirmed on your booking confirmation, receipt, or signed contract. Prices may vary by service and are subject to change for future bookings.

5. Signed service contracts

For some appointments, Blendz By Mora may send you an individual signed service contract with your name, service date, time, location, and fee filled in (for example via Square Contracts). Our standard contract language is published as a generic template so you can see how our contracts are written before you sign. See our Service Contract Template. Signed contracts work together with these Terms of Service, our Service Agreement, and our Privacy Policy.

Travel services may be available for an additional fee. Select the travel option during booking and include your location details in the notes. We will confirm availability and pricing. Full travel terms are in our Service Agreement.
