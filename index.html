About

With over 12 years of professional experience in makeup artistry, I bring expertise, creativity, and precision to every client. Based in Las Vegas, Nevada, I specialize in bridal, soft glam, and full glam makeup and am committed to enhancing your natural beauty and ensuring you look and feel confident for every occasion.

I am fluent in English and Spanish, so I can comfortably communicate with you in either language throughout your appointment. From brides on their wedding day to clients preparing for special events, I tailor each look to your unique features and vision. I primarily work in the Las Vegas area but also offer travel services—simply select the travel option during booking and include your location details in the notes.
