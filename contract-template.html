Service Contract Template

Blendz By Mora · Generic template · July 2026

This page shows how Blendz By Mora signed service contracts are typically written. It is a generic template—placeholders such as client name, service date, time, location, and fees are filled in on the individual contract sent to you (for example via Square Contracts). This template works together with our Terms of Service, Service Agreement, and Privacy Policy.

1. Invoices

A 50% deposit is due at booking to secure the appointment. The remaining 50% balance is due on the day of service, prior to the completion of makeup services—when Client arrives for the appointment, or for travel appointments, on the day the Artist arrives at the agreed location. The balance must be paid in full before services begin. Deposits and online payments are processed securely through Square (card or bank transfer). Payment may also be made by cash or Zelle where agreed. Services will not be performed until the deposit is received.

Card processing fee. When Client pays by credit or debit card through Square—including the booking deposit and the remaining balance invoice—a processing fee of 3.3% plus $0.30 is added to each card payment to cover Square’s transaction costs. The deposit and balance are separate card transactions; if Client pays both by card, the processing fee applies to each payment. The amount charged to Client’s card is the service portion plus the applicable fee, as shown at checkout or on the Square invoice. Bank transfer (ACH), cash, and Zelle payments are not subject to card processing fees. Bank transfers may take 2–3 business days to complete.

Refunds and Square fees. If an approved refund is issued for a payment made by credit or debit card through Square, the refund covers the service amount only (for example, the deposit or balance portion). Square card processing fees are non-refundable and are not returned when a refund is processed. This applies to each card transaction separately. Refunds for payments made by bank transfer (ACH), cash, or Zelle are not subject to card processing fees.

2. Payment methods

Payment will be made to Blendz By Mora via an approved payment card or bank transfer (ACH) (processed securely through Square on the booking page or invoice), cash, or Zelle.

3. Cancellation, rescheduling, and refunds

Cancellation and rescheduling. Client agrees to contact Blendz By Mora at BlendzByMora@gmail.com at least 72 hours before the scheduled appointment to cancel or reschedule. If Client cancels or requests to reschedule with at least 72 hours’ notice, Client may receive a full refund of the deposit (the service portion only—see Invoices regarding Square card processing fees) or reschedule to another mutually agreed date, subject to the Artist’s availability. If Client fails to attend the appointment (no-show), or cancels or requests to reschedule with less than 72 hours’ notice, the 50% deposit will be forfeited. A forfeited deposit is non-refundable and may not be applied toward a future appointment or rescheduled date.

No refunds after services are completed. Makeup artistry is a personal cosmetic service. All sales are final once services are completed—no refunds will be issued. Refunds are not provided for dissatisfaction related to factors outside the Artist’s control (including lighting, photography, or reference images that cannot be replicated exactly), or when Client has not fully disclosed known allergies, sensitivities, or skin conditions as required below.

Signed contracts may include a line for the Client to initial this section.

4. Allergies and client disclosure

Client is responsible for disclosing all known allergies, sensitivities, skin conditions, and medical concerns that may affect makeup application before services begin, including latex sensitivity, contact lens use, and recent facial treatments. Blendz By Mora is not liable for allergic reactions or irritation when Client has not fully disclosed known conditions.

5. Termination of contract

This Contract covers the agreed makeup artistry appointment and ends when services on the scheduled date are completed, unless ended earlier as described below.

Cancellations and rescheduling are governed by the Cancellation, rescheduling, and refunds section above (including the 72-hour notice requirement). That section controls if Client cancels or reschedules the appointment.

If either party ends this Contract for any other reason before services are performed, the Cancellation, rescheduling, and refunds section above applies, including forfeiture of the deposit for no-shows or cancellations with less than 72 hours’ notice.

6. Authority to sign

Each party has the authority to enter into this Contract and to perform all obligations under it.

7. Modifications

Client and Blendz By Mora must agree to any changes to this Contract in writing (including email from BlendzByMora@gmail.com).
