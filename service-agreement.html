Service Agreement

Blendz By Mora · Version July 2026 · Applies to all makeup artistry services

This Service Agreement is entered into between Blendz By Mora (“Artist”) and the client (“Client”) when the Client submits a booking request and agrees to these terms on our website. It works together with our Terms of Service, Service Contract Template, and Privacy Policy. For some appointments, a separate signed contract with your specific details may also apply.

1. Services

This agreement applies to all professional makeup artistry services offered by Blendz By Mora, including but not limited to soft glam, full glam, bridal, wedding-party, trial, and event makeup. Specific services, dates, times, and locations will be listed on the Client’s booking request and any written confirmation or receipt provided by the Artist.

2. Payment

A 50% deposit is due at booking to secure the appointment. The remaining balance is due on the day of service—when Client arrives for the appointment, or for travel appointments, on the day the Artist arrives at the agreed location. The balance must be paid in full before services begin. Payment may be made by approved payment card (Square), cash, or Zelle. The amount due is the total shown on the Client’s booking confirmation, invoice, receipt, or signed contract—not a fixed amount stated in this agreement. Prices may vary by service type and are subject to change for future bookings. See our Service Contract Template for standard signed-contract language.

Card processing fee. When Client pays by credit or debit card through Square—including the booking deposit and the remaining balance invoice—a processing fee of 3.3% plus $0.30 is added to each card payment to cover Square’s transaction costs. The deposit and balance are separate card transactions; if Client pays both by card, the processing fee applies to each payment. The amount charged to Client’s card is the service portion plus the applicable fee, as shown at checkout or on the Square invoice. Cash and Zelle payments are not subject to card processing fees.

3. Booking and confirmation

Submitting a booking request does not guarantee an appointment until the Artist confirms availability. The Artist will follow up by email or phone. Appointment times are held in the Las Vegas, Nevada area (Pacific time) unless travel arrangements are agreed in writing.

4. Cancellation and rescheduling

The Client agrees to contact Blendz By Mora at BlendzByMora@gmail.com at least 72 hours before the scheduled appointment to cancel or reschedule. If Client cancels or requests to reschedule with at least 72 hours’ notice, Client may receive a full refund of the deposit or reschedule to another mutually agreed date, subject to availability.

If Client fails to attend the appointment (no-show), or cancels or requests to reschedule with less than 72 hours’ notice, the 50% deposit will be forfeited. A forfeited deposit is non-refundable and may not be applied toward a future appointment or rescheduled date.

5. Allergies, skin conditions, and client disclosure

The Client is responsible for disclosing all known allergies, sensitivities, skin conditions, and medical concerns that may affect makeup application before services begin. This includes latex sensitivity, contact lens use, recent facial treatments, and any products that have caused reactions in the past. Failure to disclose relevant information may affect results and limits the Artist’s ability to adjust product selection.

6. Limitation of liability

Blendz By Mora is not liable for allergic reactions, irritation, breakouts, skin sensitivities, or other adverse effects arising from makeup services when the Client has not fully disclosed known allergies or sensitivities, or when such reactions occur despite professional application using industry-standard products.

The Client agrees that makeup artistry is a cosmetic service and that individual results may vary. The Artist is not responsible for dissatisfaction related to factors outside the Artist’s control, including lighting, photography, or Client-provided reference images that cannot be replicated exactly.

To the fullest extent permitted by law, the Artist’s total liability for any claim related to services under this agreement is limited to the amount paid by the Client for the specific appointment giving rise to the claim.

7. Travel policy

Local / Las Vegas area: The Artist primarily serves the Las Vegas, Nevada metropolitan area. If travel is requested within this region, the Client must select the travel option when booking and include location details in the notes. Travel fees, mileage, parking, or timing adjustments will be quoted and confirmed before the appointment is finalized. Travel is not guaranteed until confirmed by the Artist.

Out-of-area and out-of-state: For appointments outside the Las Vegas area or outside Nevada, additional terms apply. The Client agrees to provide, at the Client’s expense, reasonable lodging, airfare or transportation, and local ground transportation for the Artist as needed for the agreed services. For bridal work that includes a trial and wedding-day makeup, travel arrangements must cover both the trial and the wedding day (and rehearsal or additional days if included in the booked package), unless otherwise agreed in writing.

Travel details, schedules, and any associated costs will be confirmed in writing before services are performed. The Client is responsible for a suitable, well-lit workspace with access to electrical outlets where required.

8. Conduct and readiness

The Client agrees to arrive with a clean face unless otherwise instructed, and to be ready at the scheduled start time. Delays may reduce service time or require rescheduling. Minors must have a parent or guardian present where required by law.

9. Photos and portfolio (optional)

With the Client’s separate consent, the Artist may photograph completed work for portfolio, social media, or marketing use. The Client may decline without affecting service quality.

10. Electronic acceptance

By checking the agreement box on the booking form and submitting a request, the Client confirms they have read this Service Agreement, agree to its terms, and that the electronic acceptance is equivalent to a signature for booking purposes.
