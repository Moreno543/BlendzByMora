Frequently Asked Questions

How long do makeup services take?

Soft Glam and Full Glam typically take 60–90 minutes. Bridal services vary: bridesmaid and mother-of-the-bride glam take about 45–90 minutes each, while bridal packages (including trial and day-of) are scheduled in advance. I'll confirm timing when we confirm your appointment.

What should I wear or do to prepare?

Come with a clean face—no makeup. If you have a specific look in mind (photos, inspiration), bring references. Wear something comfortable that won't rub against your neck or face. For bridal trials, a top in a similar neckline to your wedding dress helps visualize the final look.

What is your cancellation policy?

Please contact us at least 24 hours before your appointment to cancel or reschedule. Reach out at BlendzByMora@gmail.com so we can accommodate other clients and find a new time that works for you.

Do you offer travel services? What are the fees?

Yes. I travel within the Las Vegas area and beyond. Travel fees vary by destination and distance. Select the travel option when booking and include your location in the notes—I'll confirm availability and provide a quote.

Do you do bridal trials?

Yes. Bridal trials are included in several bridal packages. A trial lets us perfect your look, test products, and ensure you feel confident before your wedding day. Book a bridal package that includes a trial, or contact me to discuss options.

What if I have allergies or sensitive skin?

Please share any allergies or sensitivities when you book. I use professional products and can adjust for sensitive skin. Disclosing this ahead of time helps me choose the right products for you.
