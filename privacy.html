Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use our website, we collect information you provide directly:

  • Booking requests: Name, email, phone number, service, date, time, travel preference, and notes. We use your phone number to contact you about your appointment (for example by phone or text) when we confirm your booking.
  • Reviews: Name, the service you’re reviewing (same options as booking), rating, review text, and optionally a photo

2. How We Store Your Data

We use the following services to operate our website:

  • Supabase — Your booking and review data is stored in a database hosted by Supabase. This includes appointment details (service, date, time, contact info, travel preference) and reviews (name, service reviewed, rating, text, and photos). Supabase is a secure, GDPR-compliant platform. Supabase Privacy Policy
  • Formspree — When you submit a booking request, the information is also sent to our email via Formspree so we can confirm your appointment. Formspree Privacy Policy

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process and confirm appointments
  • Contact you about your booking
  • Display reviews on our website (with your name and review text)
  • Improve our services

4. Data Retention

Booking records are retained for business and scheduling purposes. Reviews are displayed on our site unless you request removal.

5. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us at BlendzByMora@gmail.com.

6. Third-Party Links

Our site may link to Instagram, TikTok, Yelp, and Google. We are not responsible for their privacy practices.

7. Contact

For privacy questions, contact BlendzByMora@gmail.com.
